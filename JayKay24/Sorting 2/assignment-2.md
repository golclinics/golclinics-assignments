# Compute the time and space complexity of the 3 sort algorithms implemented today.

# The algorithms can be found [here](https://replit.com/@lakivisi/sorting-gol).

## Insertion sort

- Time - _O(n ^ 2)_
- Space - _O(1)_

## Bubble sort

- Time - _O(n ^ 2)_
- Space - _O(1)_

## Selection sort

- Time - _O(n ^ 2)_
- Space - _O(1)_
